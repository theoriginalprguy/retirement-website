Words from the desert.

Essays, observations, and dispatches from a life lived mostly outdoors and occasionally at a keyboard. Published on Substack, collected here.

I started writing more seriously after retirement -- not because I had more time, but because I finally had fewer excuses. The desert has a way of slowing things down enough to notice them. These pieces are about what I notice.

Topics wander: desert ecology, Phoenix life, the occasional dispatch from Mexico, and whatever crosses my path on a Tuesday morning at Desert Botanical Garden.
